My About

Hello,

I was born on July 15, 2002 in Istanbul / Kadıköy. After my primary and secondary education, I graduated from Kadıköy Göztepe Vocational and Technical Anatolian High School with an average of 88. During my high school life, I produced 3 projects to be exhibited at TUBITAK Science Fairs to represent my school. I also did my compulsory internship at SOVOS Turkey office in departments such as IT, R&D and Human Resources. Afterwards, I continue my education life with a current average of 3.15 / 4.00 by winning the Gazi University, Gazi Education Faculty, Computer and Instructional Technologies Education department at the university.

During my university life, I took an active role in the Design and Tournament departments of our society for 1.5 year during my membership in the Gazi University Electronic Sports Community Board of Directors. I also work as a Communication Manager in my school's Erasmus Student Network (ESN) community. In addition to all these, I am a school representative in the Gaming Opinion Leader program in Red Bull TR.

In the esports industry, where I have been involved as a player since a young age, I have moved to the organizer side and currently work as a Project Manager at EMBED. As a result of my job in the company, I continue to follow up and ensure the continuity of all the projects that we have received or will receive.
